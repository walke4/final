When buying from resellers on Ebay, Grailed, and Poshmark, you should always check out the seller rating, and read the reviews. If the seller doesn't have much feedback, it's a "buy at your own risk" situation. When reading reviews, you want to check for specific words such as "authentic". Sites like Goat and Stockx check authenticity before shipping to you, but I have more information about checking for authenticity on the "Is it real or fake?" page.

What to Check for when buying from Live Auctions

This mainly applies to TikTok auctions, as that is what I use. When deciding to bid in TikTok auctions, the main thing you want to check for are seller ratings. READ THE REVIEWS. You want some of the reviews to mention the words "authentic" or "real". It is also a better bet to buy from sellers that are labeled Gold star sellers or silver star sellers as that adds an extra layer of security. TikTok is good at banning sellers that sell fake items, but new sellers might be able to slip through the cracks for a little while.
